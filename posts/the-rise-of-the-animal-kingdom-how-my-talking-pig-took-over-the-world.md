</figure><p>It all started with a talking pig. Not just any talking pig — my pet, Porky, who somehow knew all my secrets and had an unfortunate habit of revealing them to strangers.</p><p>I should have suspected something was off. Porky had always been… different. He would stare at people like he understood them, sigh dramatically when I made bad decisions, and somehow always found my hidden snacks. But I chalked it up to him being an unusually smart pig.</p><p>Then, one day, as we strolled through the city, Porky casually blurted out my childhood crush’s name to a passing businessman.</p><p>The man froze, adjusted his earpiece, and whispered something into his collar.</p><p>Turns out, he wasn’t a businessman. He was a secret agent. And he thought Porky was an advanced AI experiment that had escaped.</p><p><strong>“Sir, we need to confiscate your asset,”</strong> the agent said, reaching for his belt.</p><p>I glanced at Porky. “Talk to me, buddy.”</p><p>To my horror, Porky suddenly started speaking in a language I didn’t recognize — something between ancient Latin and garbled pig squeals.</p><p>I blinked. “Ah, I see… It’s not AI. It’s an alien.”</p><p>The agent hesitated. “An alien?”</p><p>I leaned in, lowering my voice. “And I’ll tell you everything — but only if you make me an agent too.”</p><p>A long pause. Then he handed me a black card.</p><p><strong>“Welcome to the agency.”</strong></p>
