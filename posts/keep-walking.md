This is the essence of resilience.

Progress isn’t measured by how solved you feel, but by how far you keep moving.

The walking itself is the answer.

—

Life is not a test with an answer key.  
It is a journey to be experienced.

The peace you see in the Mirror of Erised is not the peace of a life without problems,  
but the peace of a man who is no longer terrified by them.

The answer to life isn’t hidden in complex theories or new techniques.

_It lies in_ the simple, profound, and unstoppable truth of taking the next step.

**_So keep walking._**
