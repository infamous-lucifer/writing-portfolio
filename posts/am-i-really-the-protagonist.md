graf-after--p">Different backgrounds.<br>Different battles.<br>Different strengths.<br>Different timelines.</p><p name="0d47" id="0d47" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And yet, all of us — without do you?</p><p name="a59a" id="a59a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because sometimes it feels like you're not the author —<br>you're the blank face in someone else's chapter.<br>Not even a side character. Just a placeholder.</p><p name="033c" id="033c" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">It's not like you haven’t done anything.<br>You are doing things. You’re trying.<br>You will keep trying.</p><p name="48c7" id="48c7" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But when you're sprinting and others are soaring —<br>when you’re trapped in constancy<br>and they keep crossing milestones —<br>something inside you quietly asks:</p><p name="995e" id="995e" class="graf graf--p graf--startsWithDoubleQuote graf-after--p">“Is this my story… or just a role I’ve been handed?”</p><p name="0d5a" id="0d5a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">These aren’t jealous thoughts.<br>They’re not even resentful.<br>They’re just honest.</p><p name="8fcd" id="8fcd" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Maybe they’ve done more than you.<br>Maybe not.</p><p name="38f4" id="38f4" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But in the quiet —<br>in the stillness between steps —<br>you feel it:</p><p name="5a47" id="5a47" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">You’re not there.<br>Not yet.<br>Not enough.<br>Not… you.</p>
