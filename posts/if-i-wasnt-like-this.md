</figure><p>If I wasn’t like this,<br>if I didn’t feel so much,<br>want so much,<br>need so much — <br>maybe I’d finally be okay.</p><p>If I didn’t crave a girlfriend,<br>didn’t feel this ache to be loved, seen, chosen — <br>maybe I wouldn’t come off desperate.<br>Maybe I’d be calm.<br>Collected.<br>Like the ones who don’t try so hard<br>because they don’t care as much.<br>Because they never needed love to breathe.</p><p>If I didn’t carry this anxiety — <br>in my voice, my hands, my timing,<br>my silences that come too early or too late — <br>maybe I’d be better with people.<br>Maybe I’d know what to say.<br>Maybe I wouldn’t second-guess every moment<br>and punish myself after.</p><p>If I had a good mindset — <br>like they say everyone can have — <br>I wouldn’t be here.<br>Wallowing.<br>Looping.<br>Hating how I feel,<br>but unable to feel anything else.</p><p>If I wasn’t me,<br>maybe I’d be grateful.<br>Maybe I’d be light.<br>Maybe I’d know how to stay happy<br>without breaking open every few weeks<br>under the weight of my own thoughts.</p><p>If I wasn’t like this…</p><p>…but I am.</p><p>And I don’t know if I’m wrong.<br>I just know it hurts.<br>That I can’t just flip a switch and be better.<br>That my need to love and be loved<br>feels like both a flame and a curse.<br>That I want peace,<br>but my mind makes war out of every quiet moment.</p><p>If I wasn’t like this,<br>I’d be someone else.<br>Someone simpler.<br>Maybe happier.<br>But not me.</p><p>And for now — <br>all I can do is try<br>to make peace<br>with this version of myself<br>who feels too much<br>and still hopes anyway.</p>
