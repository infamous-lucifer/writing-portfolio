</figure><p>This world is not easy.<br>Not for me.</p><p>One moment, I feel joy.<br>The next — nothing.<br>Is that life?<br>Or will it be death that finally offers peace?</p><p>I love someone, deeply — <br>Then I’m scared.<br>Not of them.<br>But of myself.</p><p>I don’t hate. I know I don’t.<br>But why can’t I love — <br>even me?</p><p>I know I want for myself.<br>But somewhere, I feel selfish for it.<br>Somewhere else, I wonder if I even have a chance.</p><p>Do I want too much?<br>Or do I want too little — <br>and even that is too much for this world?</p><p>No one forced me into this wanting.<br>I could quit.<br>I could stop hoping.<br>And the world would move on,<br>unmoved.</p><p>But I won’t.<br>I can’t.</p><p>And because I want — <br>it hurts.</p>
