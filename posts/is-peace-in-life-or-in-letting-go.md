Other days, even breathing feels like a burden I can’t&nbsp;name.</h4><figure name="7c13" id="7c13" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*h3lxa1mXxocuTy80" data-width="3072" data-height="4096" data-unsplash-photo-id="7jEMbQu3p5E" data-is-featured="true" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*h3lxa1mXxocuTy80"><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a href="https://unsplash.com/@ad_17?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@ad_17?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Anshuman Debashis</a> on&nbsp;<a href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p name="9c93" id="9c93" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">This world is not easy.<br>Not for me.</p><p name="8941" id="8941" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">One moment, I feel joy.<br>The next — nothing.<br>Is that life?<br>Or will it be death that finally offers peace?</p><p name="8d38" id="8d38" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I love someone, deeply — <br>Then I’m scared.<br>Not of them.<br>But of myself.</p><p name="a3f0" id="a3f0" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I don’t hate. I know I don’t.<br>But why can’t I love — <br>even me?</p><p name="61db" id="61db" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I know I want for myself.<br>But somewhere, I feel selfish for it.<br>Somewhere else, I wonder if I even have a chance.</p><p name="8ce6" id="8ce6" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Do I want too much?<br>Or do I want too little — <br>and even that is too much for this world?</p><p name="65c7" id="65c7" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">No one forced me into this wanting.<br>I could quit.<br>I could stop hoping.<br>And the world would move on,<br>unmoved.</p><p name="9051" id="9051" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But I won’t.<br>I can’t.</p><p name="90bb" id="90bb" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">And because I want — <br>it hurts.</p>
