This world is not easy.  
Not for me.

One moment, I feel joy.  
The next — nothing.  
Is that life?  
Or will it be death that finally offers peace?

I love someone, deeply —   
Then I’m scared.  
Not of them.  
But of myself.

I don’t hate. I know I don’t.  
But why can’t I love —   
even me?

I know I want for myself.  
But somewhere, I feel selfish for it.  
Somewhere else, I wonder if I even have a chance.

Do I want too much?  
Or do I want too little —   
and even that is too much for this world?

No one forced me into this wanting.  
I could quit.  
I could stop hoping.  
And the world would move on,  
unmoved.

But I won’t.  
I can’t.

And because I want —   
it hurts.
