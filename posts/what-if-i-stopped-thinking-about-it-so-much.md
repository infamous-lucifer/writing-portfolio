Working. Learning. Showing up.<br>Maybe not shining in every area,<br>but I’m not collapsing either.</p><p name="f09b" id="f09b" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So why does this one area — <br>love, connection, companionship — <br>get to define everything?</p><p name="1e71" id="1e71" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Maybe the thinking itself is the trap.<br>The constant inner debates.<br>The association with social anxiety,<br>with labels, with pain and stories<br>I keep retelling until they become cages.</p><p name="77aa" id="77aa" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What if I chose something else?</p><p name="d87c" id="d87c" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What if I stopped trying to fix what’s missing<br>and started noticing what’s whole?</p><p name="863f" id="863f" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What if — even if it felt fake at first — <br>I wrote about the things I like about myself?</p><p name="44ef" id="44ef" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What if I named the parts that make me proud,<br>even if they sound like bragging?<br>Even if the voice inside calls it “narcissistic”?</p><p name="378a" id="378a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because maybe it’s not.<br>Maybe it’s repair.</p><p name="9376" id="9376" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Maybe I need a story where I’m not just<br>the wounded boy<br>but the man who’s trying.<br>The man who’s more than pain.<br>The man who is building something — <br>even if he still breaks sometimes.</p><p name="83ce" id="83ce" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">And maybe…<br>maybe that version deserves a voice, too.</p>
