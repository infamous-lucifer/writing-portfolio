The Question at the&nbsp;Core<p id="94f8" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">Why am I in such perpetual doubt of myself?<p id="2cbc" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Even when life is quiet — <br>no sadness, no urgency — <br>I still feel like something’s wrong.<h4 id="d219" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p">II. The Pressure to&nbsp;Rush<p id="62ad" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">We live in a world of constant comparison.<br>Even if I try to slow down, I can’t.<br>The moment I pause, I feel behind.<p id="6414" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So I keep trying to fast-track life — <br>as if finishing everything quickly<br>would finally make me feel free.<p id="2295" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But I don’t want to rush.<br>I want to live.<br>Freely. Passionately. Without fear.<h4 id="b3ef" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p">III. The Restlessness Inside&nbsp;Me<p id="c790" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">At the gym, I feel like everyone’s watching me — <br>judging my body, my form, my right to be there.<p id="4061" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">In conversations, I don’t know how to enter.<br>I keep trying to insert myself<br>just so I don’t disappear.<p id="9570" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Even in public spaces, I feel like<br>I walk like I’m confident — <br>&nbsp;but I’m terrified someone will see I’m not.<h4 id="74ef" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p">IV. The Ache for&nbsp;Love<p id="bd71" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">I just want to be loved.<br>To be held without needing to earn it.<br>To be allowed to fall apart<br>in someone’s arms — <br>and still be seen as whole.<p id="0518" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But I don’t believe I’m curable.<br>Not in the way movies promise.<br>Not in the way self-help books say.<h4 id="dae4" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p">V. The Shame Around Capabilities<p id="4ea9" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">I don’t trust my abilities.<br>Reading? 15 minutes max.<br>Talking? I listen, but I have nothing to say.<p id="e99e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Even shows I want to watch — <br>I get bored. I spiral. I return to overthinking.<p id="be05" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Even with all this awareness — <br>I still feel stuck.<h4 id="b54c" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p">VI. The Quiet Confession<p id="a3b3" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">I’m in my early 20s,<br>but it feels like I’ve already failed.<p id="9775" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I didn’t go to that prestigious college.<br>I spent two years wandering, trying different paths.<br>Now I’m here — trying again.<br>But I’m tired. And unsure.<h4 id="2616" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--p">VII. The&nbsp;Loop<p id="fd49" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h4">I see couples online.<br>I imagine love with strangers.<br>Not because I know them — <br>but because I want to feel loved by someone.<p id="5c57" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">It doesn’t help.<br>It only deepens the loneliness.
