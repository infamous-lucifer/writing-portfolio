beautiful, funny, kind — <br>and many things I don’t yet know.<br>I caught it in the imagining.</p><p name="7dfc" id="7dfc" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What do you do<br>when you catch feelings<br>too much,<br>too soon<br>for someone?</p><p name="e246" id="e246" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">How do you hold<br>when a crush forms<br>before the friendship does — <br>just two, three weeks,<br>not enough to know them,<br>but enough to want them?</p><p name="3695" id="3695" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">When you crave their presence,<br>when their absence carves<br>a hollow under your ribs,<br>heavier than it should?<br>When you fidget near them,<br>heart folding inward,<br>too shy to speak,<br>too loud to ignore?</p><p name="090e" id="090e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I tried running, freezing,<br>silencing it — <br>my breath caught<br>like frost on a winter pane.</p><p name="6a28" id="6a28" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Tried killing it,<br>calling it fantasy.</p><p name="932a" id="932a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because I know<br>I caught this crush<br>in the act of imagining.</p><p name="d2e9" id="d2e9" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But what do you do<br>when even fantasy<br>demands a voice?<br>When you ache<br>just to sit near them,<br>to listen,<br>to watch them speak — <br>and that alone<br>feels like a kind of love?</p><p name="b67e" id="b67e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">There are two voices in me.</p><p name="2288" id="2288" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">One says:<br><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">You like her.<br>It’s soft, it’s pure.<br>Let it be.<br>Tell her — <br>you have a crush on her.</em></p><p name="ca45" id="ca45" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">The other yanks the brake:<br><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">No.<br>Too soon.<br>Too much.<br>You’ll scare her.<br>She’ll pull away.</em></p><p name="8694" id="8694" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And maybe — <br>maybe she doesn’t think of me that way.<br>I feel it<br>when I see her laugh with others.<br>Not quite jealousy,<br>but a reminder:<br>she might never see me like that.<br>She might already<br>be loving someone else.</p><p name="f53b" id="f53b" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So what do you do — <br>when you catch feelings<br>too much,<br>too soon,<br>for someone?</p><p name="5dd2" id="5dd2" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">When your heart opens its hands<br>but the timing<br>doesn’t know how to hold?</p><p name="efa8" id="efa8" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">What am I most afraid of losing<br>by telling her?</p><p name="ed31" id="ed31" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Is it:<br>Her interest?<br>My sense of dignity<br>if she doesn’t feel the same?<br>The fragile illusion<br>that maybe<br>it could still become something?</p><p name="4e31" id="4e31" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">All.<br>I am afraid because of all.</p><p name="81b6" id="81b6" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And I don’t know what each voice<br>inside me<br>truly wants to give — <br>because I’ve never been here before.</p><p name="3e12" id="3e12" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I don’t know how to love.<br>I don’t know how to be a boyfriend.<br>How to chase,<br>how to stay,<br>how to behave inside a relationship.<br>This might be my first.<br>And that terrifies me.</p><p name="6587" id="6587" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Is it even presumptuous<br>to think I could be that for someone?<br>To even <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">imagine</em> it?</p><p name="8543" id="8543" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But maybe…</p><p name="7171" id="7171" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Maybe not knowing what I’m doing<br>is exactly why<br>I need to start learning.</p><p name="112e" id="112e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Maybe I’ll never feel ready.<br>Maybe there is no mental rehearsal<br>that prepares you<br>for the tremble in your voice<br>when someone makes you feel this much.</p><p name="72fb" id="72fb" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So I must learn how to love<br>by trying,<br>failing,<br>repairing,<br>and daring again.</p><p name="116f" id="116f" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Maybe the first lesson in love is this:</p><blockquote name="26e0" id="26e0" class="graf graf--blockquote graf-after--p graf--trailing"><em class="markup--em markup--blockquote-em">Loving someone isn’t about doing it perfectly.<br>It’s about choosing to show up<br>even when you don’t know how.</em></blockquote>
