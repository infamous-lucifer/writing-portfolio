Overly eager.<br>Always trying to join.<br>Trying to be their friend.<br>Making jokes — the kind that land badly or don’t land at all.</p><p name="60dd" id="60dd" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I try to be the clown.<br>Maybe I am the clown.<br>So they like me.<br>So they include me.<br>So they choose me.</p><p name="5195" id="5195" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But underneath that performance, I’m scared.<br>Because if I don’t do all this — <br>If I don’t over-try — <br>I fear they won’t see me at all.</p><p name="15f5" id="15f5" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I fear I won’t have anything to offer.<br>Not resonance. Not connection.<br>Not the depth people admire.<br>Not the cleverness that earns respect.<br>Not the strength or confidence that makes people listen.</p><p name="57b4" id="57b4" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So I try too hard, because if I stop trying — <br>what’s left?</p><p name="9b70" id="9b70" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">A quiet boy with no opinion, no charisma,<br>no edge,<br>no voice that naturally fits into group conversations.</p><p name="482f" id="482f" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I sit beside them and wonder:<br>what do they see?</p><p name="ba64" id="ba64" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">That guy who jokes too much?<br>Who laughs too loudly at the wrong things?<br>Who seems desperate to be liked — <br>but isn’t?</p><p name="22c3" id="22c3" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And worst of all…<br>I see it too.<br>And I don’t know how to stop.</p><p name="0912" id="0912" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because this is the only version of me<br>that knows how to reach out.</p><p name="112d" id="112d" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">Even if he’s reaching with a trembling hand,<br>in a costume stitched together<br>from all the things I wish I could be.</p>
