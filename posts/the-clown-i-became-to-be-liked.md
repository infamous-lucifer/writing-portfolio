<h3 id="2e75" class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading graf--title">The Clown I Became to Be&nbsp;Liked<h4 id="a13c" class="graf graf--h4 graf-after--h3 graf--subtitle">What if the only version of me they notice is the one I don’t&nbsp;respect?<figure id="214d" class="graf graf--figure graf-after--h4"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="0*eEZ-vvXDeKyzEvkX" data-width="4016" data-height="6016" data-unsplash-photo-id="pLeDOWSXNk8" data-is-featured="true" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*eEZ-vvXDeKyzEvkX"><figcaption class="imageCaption">Photo by <a href="https://unsplash.com/@dentrushtin?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" data-href="https://unsplash.com/@dentrushtin?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="photo-creator noopener" target="_blank">Den Trushtin</a> on&nbsp;<a href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" data-href="https://unsplash.com?utm_source=medium&amp;utm_medium=referral" class="markup--anchor markup--figure-anchor" rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p id="7a98" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">I think I need to confess my behavior.<p id="54f6" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Overly casual. Overly eager.<br>Always trying to join.<br>Trying to be their friend.<br>Making jokes — the kind that land badly or don’t land at all.<p id="60dd" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I try to be the clown.<br>Maybe I am the clown.<br>So they like me.<br>So they include me.<br>So they choose me.<p id="5195" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But underneath that performance, I’m scared.<br>Because if I don’t do all this — <br>If I don’t over-try — <br>I fear they won’t see me at all.<p id="15f5" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I fear I won’t have anything to offer.<br>Not resonance. Not connection.<br>Not the depth people admire.<br>Not the cleverness that earns respect.<br>Not the strength or confidence that makes people listen.<p id="57b4" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So I try too hard, because if I stop trying — <br>what’s left?<p id="9b70" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">A quiet boy with no opinion, no charisma,<br>no edge,<br>no voice that naturally fits into group conversations.<p id="482f" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I sit beside them and wonder:<br>what do they see?<p id="ba64" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">That guy who jokes too much?<br>Who laughs too loudly at the wrong things?<br>Who seems desperate to be liked — <br>but isn’t?<p id="22c3" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And worst of all…<br>I see it too.<br>And I don’t know how to stop.<p id="0912" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because this is the only version of me<br>that knows how to reach out.<p id="112d" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">Even if he’s reaching with a trembling hand,<br>in a costume stitched together<br>from all the things I wish I could be.
