graf-after--p">I know you’re trying to help me.<br>You’ve always tried.</p><p name="8633" id="8633" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">You speak when I’m about to hit “publish,”<br>when someone I know softness got met with smirks or shrugs or nothing at all.</p><p name="232a" id="232a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And you vowed: <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Never again.</em><br>Never let them see that part of us.<br>Never let them get close enough to hurt it.</p><p name="6c31" id="6c31" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And for a while, you were right.<br>You kept me hidden.<br>Safe.<br>Small.<br>Unchanged.</p><p name="0fb6" id="0fb6" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But now?</p><p name="3604" id="3604" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I’m not a child trying to survive schoolyards.<br>I’m not a teenager trying to decode stares.<br>I’m someone who writes — who <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">needs</strong> to write — because it’s the only way to stay real.</p><p name="cc8a" id="cc8a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And yes, I still feel afraid.<br>Afraid that they’ll judge me.<br>Afraid that I’m too much and not enough at the same time.</p><p name="d90b" id="d90b" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But here’s what you don’t see:</p><p name="0b24" id="0b24" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I’ve survived their silence.<br>I’ve survived their misunderstanding.<br>And still, I have something worth saying.</p><p name="0448" id="0448" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So to you, my scared voice — <br>You can stay.<br>You can whisper.<br>You can tremble in the corner when I speak out.</p><p name="1c44" id="1c44" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But you don’t get to hold the pen anymore.</p><p name="30f1" id="30f1" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I write now.</p><p name="5279" id="5279" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And I write not because it’s safe,<br>but because it’s <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">true</em>.</p><p name="eb09" id="eb09" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because if I don’t,<br>I’ll lose myself in the hiding you call protection.</p><p name="7ea9" id="7ea9" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So thank you for keeping me alive.<br>But I’m here to do more than survive now.</p><p name="b8aa" id="b8aa" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I’m here to <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">be seen.</em></p><p name="48f7" id="48f7" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">— Akshat</em></p>
