rel="photo-source noopener" target="_blank">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure><p id="40be" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">Hey.<p id="0620" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I know you’re trying to help me.<br>You’ve always tried.<p id="8633" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">You speak when I’m about to hit “publish,”<br>when someone I know might see my post,<br>when I write something too raw,<br>too soft,<br>too <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">me</em>.<p id="2ce1" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">You whisper things like:<blockquote id="67c7" class="graf graf--blockquote graf--startsWithDoubleQuote graf-after--p"><em class="markup--em markup--blockquote-em">“They’ll laugh at this.”<br>“This is too much.”<br>“You’re just being needy.”<br>“You’re giving them ammunition.”</em></blockquote><p id="a39b" class="graf graf--p graf-after--blockquote">You don’t say these things because you’re cruel.<br>You say them because you’re scared.<p id="669b" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">You remember the classrooms,<br>the teasing,<br>the silence after vulnerability,<br>the way softness got met with smirks or shrugs or nothing at all.<p id="232a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And you vowed: <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">Never again.</em><br>Never let them see that part of us.<br>Never let them get close enough to hurt it.<p id="6c31" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And for a while, you were right.<br>You kept me hidden.<br>Safe.<br>Small.<br>Unchanged.<p id="0fb6" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But now?<p id="3604" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I’m not a child trying to survive schoolyards.<br>I’m not a teenager trying to decode stares.<br>I’m someone who writes — who <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">needs</strong> to write — because it’s the only way to stay real.<p id="cc8a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And yes, I still feel afraid.<br>Afraid that they’ll judge me.<br>Afraid that I’m too much and not enough at the same time.<p id="d90b" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But here’s what you don’t see:<p id="0b24" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I’ve survived their silence.<br>I’ve survived their misunderstanding.<br>And still, I have something worth saying.<p id="0448" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So to you, my scared voice — <br>You can stay.<br>You can whisper.<br>You can tremble in the corner when I speak out.<p id="1c44" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">But you don’t get to hold the pen anymore.<p id="30f1" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I write now.<p id="5279" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">And I write not because it’s safe,<br>but because it’s <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">true</em>.<p id="eb09" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Because if I don’t,<br>I’ll lose myself in the hiding you call protection.<p id="7ea9" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">So thank you for keeping me alive.<br>But I’m here to do more than survive now.<p id="b8aa" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">I’m here to <em class="markup--em markup--p-em">be seen.</em><p id="48f7" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing"><em class="markup--em markup--p-em">— Akshat</em>
